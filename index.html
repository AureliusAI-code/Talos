Many minds, one merge.

Each proposer generates a candidate independently. The aggregator doesn't average — it debates, surfacing the strongest reasoning and discarding the rest.

A dedicated verifier holds the gate: nothing ships without passing tests and a clean cross-agent review. Reliability compounds instead of hallucination accumulating.

Because the topology is dynamic, Talos scales agents to the difficulty of the task — a typo wakes one; a migration wakes the whole swarm.
